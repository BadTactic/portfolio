About Myself

I am a strategic sourcing, talent qualification and deployment professional living in the beautiful city of Portland, Oregon. My focus is largely serving the major creative organizations in the PACNW and their respective organizational initiatives, whether those be technical in nature e.g. SDLC initiatives, migrations, implementations, infrastructure overhauls, Big Data initiatives, data modeling/visualization projects, front-end development projects, creative initiatives etc. or Functional projects; such as business process engineering projects, project management operations, business analysis/business systems analysis needs, marketing strategy overhauls etc. Whatever the request, I seek the finest talent with the best cultural fit for the project. I am continuously seeking the next double coincidence of wants. My primary interests are in entrepreneurship, recruitment, photography, business organization & foundation, evolutionary psychology and ultimately working with people.

I aspire to building excellent relationships, solving problems and infiltrating/understanding complex systems. Networks are my passion, people are my bailiwick, and relationships are at the bedrock of both.
